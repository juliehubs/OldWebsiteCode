Project Description

THE CLIENT

Fandango is the "go to movie destination for more than 30 million moviegoers each month". For this mock project, Fandango requested an expansion from their existing product to include the ability to purchase tickets to concerts and local events. Fandango also requested a service to notify users of events happening in their local area, with a focus on ease of purchase and digital tickets.

THE PROPOSAL

After completing initial research and user interviews, we decided to create a focused, holistic product. This means a user is able to buy and view their ticket all while using Fandango across multiple platforms. We chose to narrow our scope away from notifications because of time constraints and consistent data showing most people find out about events through social media and word of mouth.

MY ROLE

For this team project, I focused on persona development, journey maps, site maps, user flows, iOS wireframes, and style continuity.
